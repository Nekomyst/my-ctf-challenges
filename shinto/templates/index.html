神社

In Japan, a Shinto Shrine is considered a sacred place.
The spies are behind a secret hidden in there and are trying to steal it through the Shrine App.
Can you find out the secret before the spies do?

In Japanese, a Shinto Shrine is called a Jinja (神社). Any kind of Saihai are done at the Jinja.
The Jinja is considered a sacred place, and doing wrong there, such as littering or stealing things is considered a taboo.
This is not a place for a spy who is trying to steal the secret flag.
Beware, it might be guarded by an army of AI Robots.
